Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of devices do you repair?

We repair desktops, laptops, gaming PCs, mobile phones, tablets, and Apple devices. If you're unsure, just reach out—we’ll be happy to help.

2. Do you offer on-site services?

Yes, we offer on-site support within our service area. Additional charges may apply based on location and service type.

3. How long does a typical repair take?

Repair times vary depending on the issue, but most services are completed within 1–3 business days. We'll give you an estimated turnaround after initial diagnosis.

4. Is data recovery always successful?

While we achieve a high success rate, data recovery depends on the condition of your storage device. We will assess and let you know the possibilities before proceeding.

5. Can I get remote support for software issues?

Absolutely. We provide secure remote support for issues like software installation, virus removal, system optimization, and more.

6. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, major credit/debit cards, UPI, and bank transfers. Payment is due upon completion of service.

7. Do you provide warranties on repairs?

Yes, we offer a limited warranty on most repairs and parts. Warranty details vary by service and will be discussed at the time of service.
